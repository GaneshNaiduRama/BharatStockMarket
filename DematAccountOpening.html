Angel One Account Closing Procedure Angel One offers a 2-in-1 account which includes trading and demat accounts. If you are no more making investments or trades through Angel or moved to some other broker, it is recommended to close the account to avoid unwanted penalties or misuse of your account.

Note that most Angel accounts come with Annual Maintenance Charges (AMC), which are payable even if you use them or now. The AMC keeps accumulating every year with very high interest.

Follow below simple steps to close your Angel One Account:

Visit nearby Angel One office. Clear all pending dues. Transfer or sell all your demat account holdings with Angel. Return back all unused offline demat transaction slips. Fill out the Account Closure Form and submit it to the branch. The Angel One Account Closure Form pdf can also be downloaded from Angel One website. Note:

Account closure request is not accepted through Email or Phone call. An in-person visit and physical form submission is required. If there are multiple account holders on the account, each one has to sign the account closing form. If the closure request is rejected, the customer gets an SMS on the registered mobile number. Customers who are out of India can download the form, print it, fill it, sign it, and send it to your registered branch in India. Make sure to keep a copy of your account closure request acknowledgment in at safe place until you have written confirmation that your account is closed. Many Angel customers complain that despite submitting the account closure request, they keep getting bills from Angel and later struggle to close the accounts. It's very important to follow up on account closure to avoid unwanted surprises as many a time the requests get rejected.

It usually takes 7 working days for Angel One to process the account closure request. Customers receive a confirmation letter within 15 days.

It's very important for customers to get a confirmation of the account closure to avoid surprises later. If you do not receive any information in 15 days, contact the local branch where you submitted the closure request.
